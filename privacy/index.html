MIVLA™ PRIVACY POLICY Effective date: March 31, 2026 Version 1.0 MIVLA™ is built on a single privacy principle: what you browse stays on your device. We do not upload your content, we do not store what you scan, and we do not sell your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how. 01 — WHO WE ARE MIVLA™ is an Informational Risk Alert System developed and operated by XTREME PROJECTS, Rua António Cardoso 427 H33, Porto 4150-082, Portugal. Contact: privacy@mivla.app 02 — WHAT WE COLLECT We collect the minimum data required to operate the service: Account data: email address and authentication token when you create an account or sign in with Apple Usage data: scan count (integer), subscription status, trial start date — stored in your Firestore user document Subscription data: transaction identifiers from Apple StoreKit, used to validate your subscription status Crash and performance data: anonymous crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics, containing no personally identifiable information We do not collect: the content you browse, screenshots you analyse, social media posts you view, or any data about what you scan. 03 — ON-DEVICE PROCESSING All content analysis — deepfake detection, AI-generated content identification, disinformation signals — is performed entirely on your device using Apple's CoreML framework and on-device machine learning models. Content you view through MIVLA is never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never transmitted to any third party for analysis purposes. 04 — HOW WE USE YOUR DATA To authenticate your account and maintain your session To track your scan count and manage subscription access To validate subscription receipts and prevent fraud To improve app stability via anonymous crash reports To comply with legal obligations under EU and Portuguese law We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties. 05 — THIRD-PARTY SERVICES Firebase (Google): authentication, database, crash reporting — governed by Google's Privacy Policy Apple StoreKit: subscription management — governed by Apple's Privacy Policy Google Fact Check API: used to cross-reference claims against public fact-check databases — no personal data is transmitted 06 — DATA RETENTION Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymous crash reports are retained for 90 days. 07 — YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR) As a resident of the European Union, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR): Right of access — request a copy of your personal data Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data Right to erasure — request deletion of your data Right to restriction — limit how we process your data Right to data portability — receive your data in a structured format Right to object — object to processing based on legitimate interests To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mivla.app. We will respond within 30 days. 08 — CHILDREN'S PRIVACY MIVLA is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us immediately at privacy@mivla.app. 09 — SECURITY We implement industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, Firebase security rules restricting data access to authenticated users only, and no storage of sensitive content on our servers. 10 — CHANGES TO THIS POLICY We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification.
